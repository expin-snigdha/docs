# Assets Directory

Place your Expedera logo and favicon files here:

- `expedera-logo.png` - Your company logo (recommended size: 128x128px or larger, transparent background)
- `expedera-favicon.png` - Your favicon icon (recommended size: 32x32px or 16x16px)

These files are referenced in the `mkdocs.yml` configuration:
- The logo appears in the navigation header
- The favicon appears in the browser tab
